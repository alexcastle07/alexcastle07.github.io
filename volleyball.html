About Volleyball

Volleyball is a team sport played by two teams of six players on a court divided by a net. The goal is to send the ball over the net and ground it on the other team's side. I've been playing since highschool and it's been one of the biggest parts of my life ever since.

Why I Love Volleyball

There are so many reasons I love this sport. Here are my top ones:

  1. Teamwork
    • Every point requires all 6 players working together
    • Communication is key — you have to talk constantly
    • You build really close friendships with your teammates
  2. Competition
    • Every game is different and exciting
    • Playing in tournaments pushes you to get better
  3. Physical fitness
    • Great cardio workout
    • Builds leg strength from all the jumping
    • Improves hand-eye coordination
  4. It's fast-paced
    • Rallies can go on forever
    • No clock — you play until someone wins the set

Positions on the Court

Volleyball has six positions, each with a different role:

Setter

The playmaker. Sets the ball for hitters and controls the offense.

Outside Hitter

The main attacker on the left side. Usually the team's top scorer.

Opposite Hitter

Attacks from the right side and often blocks the opposing outside hitter.

Middle Blocker

The tallest player. Leads the blocking and hits quick sets in the middle.

Libero

The defensive specialist. Wears a different color jersey and can't attack.

Defensive Specialist

Substitutes in for back-row players to strengthen the defense.
